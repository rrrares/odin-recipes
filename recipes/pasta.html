Pasta Carbonara

Spaghetti alla carbonara. Luscious and wonderfully indulgent, pasta carbonara takes as long to make as it does to cook the pasta.

The ingredients are simpleâ€”just spaghetti (or another long pasta), and the carbonara is made with pancetta or bacon, eggs, Parmesan, a little olive oil, salt and pepper.

The silky carbonara sauce is created when the beaten eggs are tossed with the hot pasta and a little fat from the pancetta or bacon.

Ingredients

  • Pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the pasta water
  2. Saute the pancetta
  3. Beat the eggs and cheese
  4. Cook the past
  5. Toss the pasta with pancetta
